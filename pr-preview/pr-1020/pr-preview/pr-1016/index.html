The Opportunity Project

The Opportunity Project (TOP) is a technology development sprint program at the U.S. Census Bureau that unlocks the value of open data for the economy and people nationwide.

Innovative Solutions to National Challenges

TOP has been instrumental in the development of many new tools that serve the public, tackling challenges like the opioid crisis, workforce development, entrepreneurship, disaster response, and rural development. Here are just a few notable examples:

  • Insights for ArcGIS by Esri

    Predicts geographic areas where opioid emergencies are likely to occur to help public safety and health officials save lives.

  • City Builder by CitiVentures

    Designed to help investors, developers, municipalities, and community members make impactful, place-based investments.

  • GreatSchools and Zillow

    Zillow integrates school ratings from GreatSchools.org, the preeminent ranking system of U.S. public schools.

  • Job Opportunity Tool by RedFin

    Helps Redfin understand the opportunities and tradeoffs in housing and employment when looking to relocate for work.

  • Floodplain Management Dashboard by Forerunner

    Enables local government floodplain managers to effectively communicate flood risk and track changes in their community.

  • Sidekick by MySidewalk

    An AI-powered data assistant that helps community leaders find, visualize, and share data to inform decisions and improve outcomes.
